About Kimi
Kimi has spent a lifetime understanding what makes a woman love her hair color - from finding the perfect, sophisticated, natural- looking shade, to the satisfaction of covering up every single grey hair. Kimi’s genius at creating perfect hair color had made him the sought-after colorist in Egypt. His expert knowledge and artistic eye mean he’s constantly called on by women who want his special brand of glossy, natural, believable-looking color. His vision and technical ability with color, along with his heart and roots at Toi, has allowed him to shape the Egyptian beauty landscape for over a decade.
